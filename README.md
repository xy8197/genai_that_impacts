# GenAI That Impacts

From GenAI hype to measurable business value.

---

## Why this repository exists

Many teams experiment with GenAI, build prototypes, and run pilots — yet struggle to translate these into meaningful business outcomes. The gap is rarely about the model. It’s about product thinking, system design, and execution.

This repository is a collection of our learnings, reflections, and frameworks as we explore:

- How to build GenAI products that actually deliver value
- How to move from prototypes to production
- How to design systems that are reliable, usable, and scalable
- How to make better decisions as an AI product leader

---

## What you'll find here

This is a “learn in public” project. Expect:

- 🧠 **Field notes** — short insights from real-world observations
- 🧩 **Frameworks** — structured thinking for AI product decisions
- 🔍 **Deep dives** — breakdowns of concepts (e.g. RAG, evaluation, UX)
- ⚙️ **Case thinking** — how AI applies to real business problems
- ❗ **Failures & trade-offs** — what doesn’t work (and why)

---

## Principles

- Focus on **business impact over hype**
- Prioritize **clarity over complexity**
- Share **thinking in progress**, not just polished answers
- Stay grounded in **real-world application**
